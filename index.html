good to know

A few quick answers

How many books at once?

Up to 4 books per registered child, each rental cycle.

How long is a cycle?

One month. Need longer? Extend any book for RM6 — just tell us 5 days before it's due.

How do delivery & returns work?

We deliver to your building's parcel area; label returns “BUKUTOPIA” and leave them at your parcel rack. No postage.

Is there a deposit?

A refundable RM60 secures your account and the books. You get it back when you stop.

Late returns?

A small RM8 per book, per month — but just message us and we'll sort it out.

Lost or damaged?

Accidents happen! A replacement fee applies based on the book's value.

How do I get started?

Browse the catalogue, pick your books, and register — we'll take it from there.

Still have questions?

Reach out to us anytime on WhatsApp and we'll be happy to help.
